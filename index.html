About Me

I’m David, a developer motivated to combine the art of design with the art of programming. With hands-on experience in crafting user interfaces using React and other Front-End tools, I enjoy tackling challenges and turning ideas into reality.

My approach is methodical and thorough, and I'm always looking for ways to improve processes and streamline workflows.

Outside of work, I enjoy playing videogames or just reading. I’m always eager to learn and grow, share and meet with new people or collaborate on exciting projects.

All Sports - Admin

Project Overview:

In this project, I contributed to the development of the Administrators, a critical component of the All Sports digital ecosystem.

Technical Highlights:

  • Frontend Development: Using React, leveraging Redux for state management and Material-UI for a responsive design
  • Backend Development: Node.js with Express, and Firestore as the database management system
  • Authentication: Uses Firebase authentication

Smile Up

Project Overview:
